# Requirements Document: SignalFlow GTM Intelligence Engine

## Introduction

SignalFlow is a GTM Intelligence Engine that helps early-stage founders systematically discover high-fit prospects, generate personalized outreach messages, track conversations, and extract structured insights from customer interactions. The MVP focuses on enabling founders to get to product-market signal faster by facilitating high-quality 1:1 conversations with the right people.

This requirements document covers the MVP scope: lead list with scoring, AI-powered message generation, manual outreach tracking, a simple CRM, and post-call structured insight capture. Full automation, complex ML models, multi-channel orchestration, and deep integrations are explicitly out of scope.

## Glossary

- **Lead**: A prospective person identified as a potential conversation partner for the founder
- **ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)**: A description of the characteristics that define the best-fit prospects for the founder's product
- **Lead_Score**: A numeric ranking (1–100) representing how well a Lead matches the founder's ICP based on relevance, activity, and intent signals
- **Outreach_Message**: A personalized message (cold DM or email) generated by the system for a specific Lead
- **CRM_Status**: The current state of a Lead in the outreach pipeline, one of: New, Contacted, Replied, Booked, or Closed
- **Call_Note**: A structured record of insights captured after a conversation, including pain points, objections, and feature requests
- **Dashboard**: The primary interface where the founder views lead activity, outreach status, and conversation insights
- **Lead_List**: A collection of Leads with associated Lead_Scores, filterable and sortable by the founder
- **Enrichment_Data**: Contextual information scraped from public sources (LinkedIn bio, Twitter posts, company info) used to personalize Outreach_Messages
- **Message_Generator**: The AI-powered component that produces Outreach_Messages using LLM and Enrichment_Data
- **Insight_Extractor**: The component that transforms raw Call_Notes into structured tags and patterns
- **Throttle_Limit**: The maximum number of outreach actions permitted per day to prevent spam behavior

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: ICP Definition

**User Story:** As a founder, I want to define my Ideal Customer Profile, so that the system can identify and score leads that match my target audience.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Dashboard SHALL provide a form for the founder to define an ICP with the following fields: target role, industry, company stage, geography, and custom tags
2. WHEN the founder submits an ICP definition, THE Dashboard SHALL validate that at least target role and industry are provided
3. WHEN the founder saves an ICP, THE Dashboard SHALL persist the ICP and use it as the basis for Lead_Score calculations
4. WHEN the founder updates an existing ICP, THE Dashboard SHALL recalculate all Lead_Scores in the Lead_List within 30 seconds
5. IF the founder submits an ICP with missing required fields, THEN THE Dashboard SHALL display a specific validation error indicating which fields are missing

### Requirement 2: Lead Discovery and Enrichment

**User Story:** As a founder, I want to discover and enrich leads from public sources, so that I have a pool of high-fit prospects to reach out to.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the founder triggers a lead discovery search, THE Lead_List SHALL return leads matching the active ICP from supported public data sources
2. THE Lead_List SHALL display each Lead with: name, role, company, industry, geography, and Lead_Score
3. WHEN a Lead is added to the Lead_List, THE Lead_List SHALL enrich the Lead with Enrichment_Data from available public profiles within 10 seconds
4. IF a public data source is unavailable during enrichment, THEN THE Lead_List SHALL mark the Lead as "partially enriched" and display which data sources failed
5. THE Lead_List SHALL support manual Lead entry where the founder provides name, role, company, and any additional context

### Requirement 3: Lead Scoring

**User Story:** As a founder, I want leads scored by fit and relevance, so that I can prioritize outreach to the highest-probability prospects.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Lead is added or enriched, THE Lead_List SHALL calculate a Lead_Score from 1 to 100 based on ICP match, role relevance, and available intent signals
2. THE Lead_List SHALL sort Leads by Lead_Score in descending order by default
3. WHEN the founder filters the Lead_List by minimum Lead_Score, THE Lead_List SHALL display only Leads at or above the specified threshold
4. WHEN the ICP is updated, THE Lead_List SHALL recalculate all Lead_Scores to reflect the new ICP criteria
5. THE Lead_List SHALL display a breakdown of the Lead_Score showing individual factor contributions (ICP match, role relevance, intent signals)

### Requirement 4: Personalized Outreach Message Generation

**User Story:** As a founder, I want AI-generated outreach messages personalized to each lead's context, so that my outreach feels human, specific, and timely.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the founder requests an Outreach_Message for a Lead, THE Message_Generator SHALL produce a personalized message using the Lead's Enrichment_Data and the founder's product context
2. THE Message_Generator SHALL support two message types: cold email and cold DM
3. WHEN generating an Outreach_Message, THE Message_Generator SHALL reference at least one specific detail from the Lead's Enrichment_Data (recent post, bio detail, or company context)
4. THE Message_Generator SHALL present the generated Outreach_Message in an editable text field before the founder sends or copies the message
5. WHEN the founder selects a tone preference (professional, casual, or direct), THE Message_Generator SHALL adjust the Outreach_Message style accordingly
6. IF the Lead has insufficient Enrichment_Data for personalization, THEN THE Message_Generator SHALL generate a message using available data and flag the message as "limited personalization"
7. THE Message_Generator SHALL produce Outreach_Messages of 150 words or fewer for cold DMs and 250 words or fewer for cold emails

### Requirement 5: Outreach Tracking

**User Story:** As a founder, I want to manually track my outreach activities, so that I know which leads I have contacted and can follow up systematically.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the founder marks a Lead as contacted, THE Dashboard SHALL record the outreach date, channel (email or DM), and the Outreach_Message used
2. THE Dashboard SHALL display a chronological outreach history for each Lead showing all recorded outreach activities
3. WHEN the founder logs a follow-up action for a Lead, THE Dashboard SHALL append the follow-up to the Lead's outreach history with a timestamp
4. THE Dashboard SHALL provide a filtered view showing all Leads with outreach older than 7 days and no recorded reply
5. IF the founder attempts to record outreach for a Lead that exceeds the Throttle_Limit, THEN THE Dashboard SHALL display a warning and prevent the action

### Requirement 6: Simple CRM Pipeline

**User Story:** As a founder, I want a simple CRM to track lead status through my outreach pipeline, so that I can manage conversations and measure progress.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Dashboard SHALL display all Leads in a pipeline view with columns for each CRM_Status: New, Contacted, Replied, Booked, and Closed
2. WHEN the founder changes a Lead's CRM_Status, THE Dashboard SHALL update the pipeline view and record the status change with a timestamp
3. THE Dashboard SHALL display aggregate counts for each CRM_Status column
4. WHEN the founder moves a Lead to "Booked" status, THE Dashboard SHALL prompt the founder to enter a meeting date and time
5. THE Dashboard SHALL support filtering the pipeline by CRM_Status, Lead_Score range, and date of last activity
6. IF the founder moves a Lead backward in the pipeline (e.g., from Booked to Replied), THEN THE Dashboard SHALL require a reason for the status change

### Requirement 7: Post-Call Insight Capture

**User Story:** As a founder, I want to capture structured insights after each conversation, so that I can systematically learn from every interaction and improve my approach.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the founder opens the post-call input form for a Lead, THE Insight_Extractor SHALL present a structured form with fields for: pain points, objections, feature requests, next steps, and overall sentiment (positive, neutral, negative)
2. WHEN the founder submits a Call_Note, THE Insight_Extractor SHALL parse the free-text entries and generate structured tags for each pain point, objection, and feature request
3. THE Insight_Extractor SHALL store all Call_Notes associated with the corresponding Lead and display them in reverse chronological order
4. THE Dashboard SHALL display an aggregated insights view showing the most frequently mentioned pain points, objections, and feature requests across all Call_Notes
5. WHEN the founder submits a Call_Note with the overall sentiment field empty, THE Insight_Extractor SHALL infer sentiment from the free-text content using the LLM
6. IF the Insight_Extractor fails to generate tags from a Call_Note, THEN THE Insight_Extractor SHALL store the raw text and flag the note for manual tagging

### Requirement 8: Dashboard Summary and Metrics

**User Story:** As a founder, I want a summary dashboard showing my GTM activity and key metrics, so that I can understand my progress and identify areas for improvement.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Dashboard SHALL display a weekly summary including: number of leads contacted, reply rate as a percentage, meetings booked, and conversion rate from meeting to next step
2. THE Dashboard SHALL display the total number of Leads in each CRM_Status
3. WHEN the founder opens the Dashboard, THE Dashboard SHALL load the weekly summary within 3 seconds
4. THE Dashboard SHALL display a list of upcoming booked meetings with Lead name, date, and time
5. THE Dashboard SHALL highlight Leads with a Lead_Score above 80 that have not been contacted as "high-priority suggestions"
6. WHEN the founder has fewer than 3 booked meetings in the current week, THE Dashboard SHALL display a prompt suggesting leads to contact based on Lead_Score

### Requirement 9: Spam Prevention and Rate Limiting

**User Story:** As a founder, I want built-in safeguards against spam behavior, so that my outreach remains high-quality and my accounts stay in good standing.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Dashboard SHALL enforce a configurable daily Throttle_Limit on the number of outreach actions recorded per channel (default: 20 per channel per day)
2. WHEN the founder reaches 80% of the daily Throttle_Limit, THE Dashboard SHALL display a warning indicating the remaining outreach capacity
3. WHEN the founder reaches the daily Throttle_Limit, THE Dashboard SHALL prevent recording additional outreach actions for that channel until the next calendar day
4. THE Dashboard SHALL allow the founder to configure the Throttle_Limit within a range of 5 to 50 per channel per day
5. IF the founder attempts to set a Throttle_Limit above 50, THEN THE Dashboard SHALL reject the value and display a message explaining the maximum allowed limit

### Requirement 10: Data Persistence and Integrity

**User Story:** As a founder, I want my data to be reliably stored and consistent, so that I do not lose leads, outreach history, or conversation insights.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Dashboard SHALL persist all Lead data, outreach history, Call_Notes, and CRM_Status changes to the Postgres database
2. WHEN the founder creates, updates, or deletes a Lead, THE Dashboard SHALL reflect the change in the UI within 2 seconds
3. IF a database write operation fails, THEN THE Dashboard SHALL display an error message and retain the founder's unsaved input for retry
4. THE Dashboard SHALL prevent duplicate Leads based on a combination of name and company (case-insensitive match)
5. WHEN the founder deletes a Lead, THE Dashboard SHALL soft-delete the record and allow restoration within 30 days
